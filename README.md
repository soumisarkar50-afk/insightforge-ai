# InsightForge AI 🚀

## Overview
InsightForge AI is an AI-powered Product & Customer Intelligence Agent built using Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Gemini 2.5 Flash.

The agent helps businesses and product managers analyze customer feedback and automatically generate actionable business insights.

---

## Problem Statement
Product teams spend significant time manually reviewing customer feedback, identifying pain points, prioritizing issues, and creating product roadmaps.

InsightForge AI automates this process using Generative AI.

---

## Features
- Analyze customer reviews and support tickets
- Identify customer pain points
- Categorize issues
- Prioritize problems by business impact
- Recommend product improvements
- Generate product roadmaps
- Provide executive summaries and business recommendations

---

## Tech Stack
- Google Agent Development Kit (ADK)
- Gemini 2.5 Flash
- Python
- Google Cloud Shell

---

## Example Input

Customer Reviews:
1. The app crashes during checkout.
2. Search takes too long.
3. I cannot track my order.
4. The interface is confusing.
5. Payment failures happen frequently.
6. The app logs me out repeatedly.
